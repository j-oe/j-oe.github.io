Co-Founder and CEO of plusmeta GmbH.

Doctorate degree in Computer Science (Dr.-Ing.) from University of Bremen. Dissertation subject: Optimization of semantic information access to technical documentation.

Graduated in Technical Communication (B.A.) and Communication and Media Management (M.Sc.) from Karlsruhe University of Applied Sciences.

Currently lecturer for the course SGML/XML in Technical Documentation at Technical Academy Esslingen (TAE).

Located in Karlsruhe, Germany.
